Branch Sections

Issuing Department

This is the department through which insurance documents are issued, whether the insured party is a public or private hospital or an individual.

Compensation Department

This is a section through which compensation is paid, whether the insured person was in a public or private hospital or was an individual.

Accounts and Salaries Department

This is the department through which all financial procedures are carried out, from receiving instalments to issuing documents and cheques for compensation, compiling statistics and reports, and calculating the salaries of branch employees.
